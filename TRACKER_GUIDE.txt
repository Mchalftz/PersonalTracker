WHAT IS THIS?
• Study progress tracker for Philippine Mechanical Engineers Licensure Exam
• Auto-saves all progress locally (never lost)
• Works offline - perfect for studying anywhere
• Installable as Android app

================================================================================
HOW TO USE
================================================================================

1. MARK PROGRESS
   • Check box (✓) = Topic completed
   • Enter % (0-100) = Personal study progress
   • Both track separately

2. VIEW DASHBOARD (Left side)
   • Overall Mastery = Combined progress percentage
   • Days Until Exam = Countdown to August 15, 2026
   • Focus Needed = Topics you need to work on

3. ORGANIZE TOPICS
   • Click Major Title to expand/collapse all subtopics
   • Click Subtopic to show/hide items
   • Select All checkbox = Mark entire subfolder at once

4. TAKE NOTES
   • Click "Notes" button per topic
   • Save study notes, formulas, key points
   • Auto-saves with timestamps
